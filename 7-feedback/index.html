Sharing and Feedback

Overall Evaluation

1. Working Environment
The working environment here left me with an impression of being both disciplined and closely knit. Outside of working hours, the team members are very friendly and open; but the moment work begins, the atmosphere immediately turns absolutely serious. Everyone concentrates fully on their own tasks, with almost no private chatting or use of personal phones. It is precisely this professionalism and discipline that create an ideal space, helping me maintain a high level of focus whenever I read technical documents, analyze logs, or configure systems. The only thing I found a little unfortunate is that the schedule to come to the office has to go through an approval step, so the number of days I actually got to work on-site with everyone was still quite limited.

2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin
The mentors and team admin were always very enthusiastic and dedicated in their support. What I value most is their patience: there were technical issues I hadn’t fully grasped and had to ask about several times, yet they still calmly explained every detail thoroughly until I genuinely understood. Throughout the final project, everyone always created the most favorable conditions for me to accomplish the goals I had set. There were even times late at night when I messaged them for feedback on the project, and the mentors were still willing to respond and guide me wholeheartedly. I am truly fond of, and grateful for, the time the mentors devoted to guiding me throughout this internship.

3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major
This is what I cherish most about the entire internship. The tasks assigned to me closely matched my passion and career orientation, especially the areas related to the Security Operations Center (SOC), Linux OS security administration, and incident analysis. Bringing the knowledge from my school projects into a real-world environment helped clarify many things that theory alone could not fully convey.

4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities
This internship has been a major stepping stone for both my mindset and my professional skills. I not only honed my logical analytical thinking and my ability to trace system activity, but also learned meticulousness and carefulness—elements that are vital in this field. In particular, the internship gave me a deeper and more practical understanding of the processes for managing and deploying systems in Cloud environments. Besides improving my tool-usage and technical reporting skills, the projects and knowledge I accumulated have become valuable material that contributed significantly to refining my CV-writing skills and highlighting my core competencies, so that I am ready for the career path ahead.

5. Company Culture & Team Spirit
The teamwork spirit in the team is very egalitarian and built on mutual respect. Even though I was just an intern, I was always listened to whenever I offered an analytical perspective on a risk or some abnormal sign. When there was an urgent task or a difficult problem that needed to be “dissected,” everyone was always ready to sit down together to work out a solution. The feeling of directly contributing to the overall security process of the system made me truly proud.

6. Internship Policies / Benefits
The company creates the best possible conditions for students. The flexibility in timing allowed me to complete my final projects and school reports well while still keeping up with the progress of my work at the company.

Additional Questions

  • What did you find most satisfying during your internship?

Being able to work in a truly serious, distraction-free environment and directly “touching” real-world problems of a professional defense system. Above all is the dedicated accompaniment and guidance, day or night, from the mentors.

  • What do you think the company should improve for future interns?

The company could consider optimizing the office schedule approval process to be faster, allowing interns to have more on-site working days. Working directly at the office brings much higher interaction and learning efficiency.

  • If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?

Definitely yes. Especially for those who have a deep passion for the Cloud and prefer a highly disciplined, focused working environment. This is an ideal hands-on environment to gain practical experience and systematically hone technical thinking, thanks to the extremely dedicated guidance of the mentor team.

Suggestions & Expectations

  • Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?

I hope the office registration schedule process can be approved faster or that there could be more on-site office days to increase bonding time and work directly with the team.
